# Memorandum of Understanding

This Memorandum of Understanding (this "MOU") is effective as of ________ ("Effective Date").

**BETWEEN:**

**Silicon Craft Technology Public Company Limited** ("SIC" hereafter), a corporation organized and existing under the laws of Thailand, with its head office located at No. 40 Thetsaban Rangsan Nua Road, Ladyao, Chatuchak, Bangkok, 10900, Thailand.

**AND:**

**Midoriya Electric Co., Ltd.** ("Midoriya" hereafter), a corporation organized and existing under the laws of Japan, with its head office located at 1-2-6 Nihonbashi-muromachi, Chuo-ku, Tokyo 103-8301 Japan.

Where appropriate each of these Parties will hereinafter individually be referred to as "Party" and collectively be referred to as the "Parties".

In consideration of the covenants and conditions contained herein, the Parties hereby agree to the following:

---

## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this MOU is to establish a framework for the Parties to collaborate on conduction of marketing activities of SIC's products that are identified in Annex I (the "Products"), in Japan (the "Territory"), subject to the terms and conditions herein.

## 2. Scope of Collaboration

### 2.1 Marketing Responsibility

Midoriya shall be responsible for marketing the Products in the Territory at Midoriya's sole cost and expense.

### 2.2 Publication Rights (Annual Approval)

SIC grants Midoriya a limited, revocable, non-exclusive, non-transferable, and non-sublicensable right to publish the Products details on website ________ in the Territory for a period of one (1) year at a time, subject to SIC's prior reviewed and written approval.

At the end of each annual period, Midoriya must request renewed approval from SIC to continue publishing the Products details for the following year.

SIC may withhold or revoke approval at its sole discretion.

### 2.3 No Sales Right

This MOU does not grant Midoriya the right to sell or distribute the Products, nor to act as SIC's agent or partner.

### 2.4 Phased Product Scope

The Products listed in Annex I are limited to SIC's **industrial product portfolio** (Phase 1). The Parties acknowledge that SIC's product range includes additional categories, including sensor-related products. Promotion of sensor products may be discussed and mutually agreed upon as a future phase (Phase 2), subject to the following conditions:

(a) Midoriya has demonstrated sufficient knowledge and experience in RFID technology through its Phase 1 marketing activities;

(b) The cooperation between the Parties under this MOU has been satisfactorily established; and

(c) Both Parties agree in writing to expand the scope of Annex I to include sensor products.

Any expansion of the Product scope shall be documented as a written amendment to Annex I, signed by both Parties.

## 3. Term

This MOU shall remain in effect for three (3) years from the Effective Date unless terminated earlier under Section 12.

## 4. Intellectual Property

### 4.1 Ownership

All intellectual property in the Products and SIC's trademarks and logos (the "SIC Marks") remains the exclusive property of SIC.

### 4.2 Limited License to SIC Marks

SIC grants Midoriya a limited, revocable, non-exclusive, non-sublicensable license to use the SIC Marks solely for the marketing and publication activities permitted by this MOU and only within the Territory.

### 4.3 Approval

Midoriya must submit all public-facing materials referencing SIC, the Products, or the SIC Marks to SIC for prior written approval.

### 4.4 No Registration

Midoriya shall not register or attempt to register any mark or domain name containing or resembling the SIC Marks.

### 4.5 Goodwill

All goodwill arising from Midoriya's use of the SIC Marks shall inure solely to SIC.

## 5. Marketing Conduct

Midoriya shall ensure that all marketing and publication activities comply with all applicable laws and that representations about the Products are accurate and not misleading.

## 6. Costs

All marketing and publication costs shall be borne solely by Midoriya.

## 7. Compliance

Each Party shall comply with all applicable laws, including data protection, advertising laws, and anti-bribery regulations.

## 8. Confidentiality

Parties hereby acknowledge and recognize that certain information or data to be exchanged or generated in the due course of the collaboration as set forth in this MOU may be considered sensitive and confidential, or eventually be related to intellectual property rights or any of the Parties, including their respective affiliates, representatives, and successors.

The Parties agree that without prior written consent obtained from the other Parties, none of the Parties herein shall use, disclose or openly share any confidential information or intellectual property of any of the other Parties, including but not limited to any commercial, industrial, technical data and information, discoveries, ideas, concepts, know-how, techniques, specification, process, documents, information, blueprints, tracings, diagrams, models, samples, flow charts, computer programs, marketing plans, customer names data, drawing, designs, methods, business studies, whether in writing, orally, in the form of any documentary or electronic form and/or samples, models, regardless of format and the existence of any express indication as to the confidential nature of the information contained therein.

In any case, the confidentiality obligation imposed by this Section shall survive the termination or expiry of this MOU for a period of three (3) years from the termination or expiry of this MOU (as the case may be).

## 9. Non-Binding Nature

This MOU is non-exclusive and does not create any partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship.

## 10. Warranties and Liability

SIC disclaims all warranties except as expressly provided. Neither Party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages.

## 11. Indemnification

Midoriya shall indemnify SIC for claims arising from Midoriya's marketing activities, breach of this MOU, non-compliance with law, or misuse of SIC Marks.

## 12. Termination

Either Party may terminate this MOU for convenience with thirty (30) days' written notice.

Either Party may terminate immediately for material breach.

Upon termination, Midoriya shall stop all publication, cease all use of SIC Marks, and references to SIC and the Products.

## 13. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

This MOU is governed by Thai law. Both Parties shall strive to settle any dispute or claims arising from the interpretation or performance of the terms of this MOU through friendly good faith consultations. In case no settlement can be reached through consultations within twenty (20) days of the submission of such matter by one Party to the other, both Parties agree to, within thirty (30) days, submit the matter to the Thailand Arbitration Center ("THAC") to be settled under its arbitration rules by three (3) arbitrators. Two of whom shall be chosen by each of the Parties and the third arbitrator shall be chosen by the two arbitrators. The arbitration proceedings shall be conducted in English and shall take place in Bangkok. The arbitral award shall be final and binding upon both Parties and shall be enforceable in accordance with its terms.

## 14. Miscellaneous

Neither this MOU nor any portion of its rights or obligations shall be assigned or otherwise transferred by either Party without the prior written consent of the other Party. This MOU shall be binding and inure to the benefit of the Parties and their respective successors and permitted assigns.

This MOU contains the entire MOU between the Parties with respect to the subject matter thereof and supersedes all prior written and oral MOU, contract or agreement.

If any Section of this MOU is found to be illegal or unenforceable, the other Sections shall remain effective and enforceable to the greatest extent permitted by law.

No Section of this MOU may be waived except by a writing executed by the Party against whom the waiver is to be effective. A Party's failure to enforce any Section of this MOU shall neither be construed as a waiver of the provision nor prevent such Party from enforcing any other provision of this MOU.

No Section of this MOU may be amended or otherwise modified except by a writing signed by the Parties to this MOU.

This MOU may be executed in counterparts, each of which is deemed an original, but all of which together constitute one and the same MOU. In the event that any signature is delivered by an electronic signature process, such signature shall create a valid and binding obligation of the Party executing with the same force and effect as if such signature were a manually signed original thereof.

## Annex I — Products

The following SIC products are authorized for marketing by Midoriya under this MOU. All products listed below fall within SIC's **industrial product portfolio** (Phase 1).

| Product Family | Application Category | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Low-Frequency RFID Transponder IC (125 kHz) | Access Control | Contactless identification IC for access control systems (e.g., E-Garde) |
| Low-Frequency RFID Transponder IC with AES Encryption | Access Control | Secure contactless identification IC with AES encryption for high-security access control applications |
| HF/NFC Transponder IC (13.56 MHz) | Item-Level Authentication | NFC-based IC for item-level authentication and anti-counterfeiting solutions (e.g., Tradyn, AEGID) |
| UHF RFID IC | Access Control | Industrial RFID IC for access control and asset identification applications |
| RFID Interrogator / Reader IC | Industrial IoT | Reader IC for industrial RFID interrogation and data capture |

**Notes:**

1. Product families are listed by application category rather than by individual part number, to simplify Midoriya's customer-facing communications.
2. Midoriya shall promote the above products exclusively within the **industrial sector**, including but not limited to: access control, item-level authentication, and industrial IoT applications.
3. Midoriya shall **not** promote any product listed above under the category of **animal identification**.
4. Sensor-related products are excluded from this Annex and may be added under a future Phase 2 amendment, subject to Section 2.4.
5. SIC reserves the right to update this Annex by written notice to Midoriya, including the addition or removal of product families.
