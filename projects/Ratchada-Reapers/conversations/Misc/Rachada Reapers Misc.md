## What “high-res for printing” actually means

- **Professional print standard:** 300 DPI at final print size

Example:
- 12 inches wide × 300 DPI = **3600 pixels wide**
- 24 inches wide × 300 DPI = **7200 pixels wide**

A file can be:
- 1.6 MB **and high quality** (if compressed well)
- 20 MB **and low quality** (if pixel dimensions are small)
