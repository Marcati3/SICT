# FFHA Project Brief

## 1. Project Overview

The **Flying Farangs Hockey Association (FFHA)** -- in Thai: **สมาคมฟลายอิ้งฝรั่งฮอกกี้** -- is a non-profit ice hockey association being formally registered in Bangkok, Thailand. The project involves preparing the official founding documents required for legal registration with the Thai District Office (สำนักงานเขต) under the Thai Civil and Commercial Code.

The prior conversation (conducted on 2025-11-05 via ChatGPT) focused on:
- Translating the English founding documents into formal Thai suitable for government submission
- Producing bilingual (English-Thai) versions of the Founding Meeting Minutes and Official Bylaws
- Generating Word (.docx) files formatted per Thai administrative document standards

## 2. Key Decisions Made

### Naming and Branding
- **Official English name:** Flying Farangs Hockey Association (FFHA)
- **Official Thai name:** สมาคมฟลายอิ้งฝรั่งฮอกกี้
- **Combined format used on documents:** สมาคมฟลายอิ้งฝรั่งฮอกกี้ (Flying Farangs Hockey Association - FFHA)
- Note: There is a related entity referred to as the **Flying Farangs Hockey League (FFHL)** -- the treasurer Pascal Bhangay serves as treasurer of FFHL and was included in the bank account authorization for FFHA.

### Document Format
- **Sequential bilingual layout** (English paragraph followed immediately by Thai translation) was chosen as the submission format. This is the standard accepted by Thai District Offices for association registration.
- Font: TH SarabunPSK (Thai administrative standard)
- Thai names of foreign nationals were left in **Roman script** in the initial documents, with the intention of adjusting to Thai transliteration later.

### Organizational Structure
- **Non-profit** association -- all revenue must be used for association objectives only, never distributed to members.
- **Board of Directors (คณะกรรมการบริหาร):** Minimum 3 members; at least one must hold Thai nationality.
- **Board term:** 2 years, with re-election permitted.
- **Fiscal year:** January 1 through December 31.
- **Financial authority:** Two of three board members must co-sign for financial transactions.
- **Annual audit** required by a licensed Thai auditor.
- **AGM** must be held within 120 days of fiscal year-end.
- **EGM** can be called by board resolution or written request from at least 1/5 of total members.
- **Board meetings** at minimum every 3 months.
- **Bylaw amendments** require 2/3 vote at a general meeting, plus filing form ส.ค.2 with the District Office.
- **Dissolution:** Remaining assets go to a charity or sports organization approved by the board and the District Office.

### Registration
- Registration to be filed at the **District Office (สำนักงานเขต)** in Bangkok.
- Application form: **แบบ ส.ค.1** (Association Registration Application).
- **Phonwarit Prechawirakul** (นายพรวฤทธิ์ เพชรวีระกุล) was designated as the authorized representative to file the registration.

### Association Objectives (8 Objectives as Approved)
1. Promote, establish, and develop ice hockey in Thailand at amateur and recreational levels
2. Provide members with safe, equitable training and competition opportunities with good sportsmanship
3. Foster cooperation and unity between Thai and international athletes
4. Organize competitions, leagues, and events to develop skills and community engagement
5. Promote teamwork, respect, and fair play on and off the ice
6. Represent Thailand's ice hockey community nationally and internationally, coordinating with relevant sports agencies
7. Support activities that grow ice hockey recognition in Thailand
8. Operate as a non-profit, using all revenue exclusively for association objectives
