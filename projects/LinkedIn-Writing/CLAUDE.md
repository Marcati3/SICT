## Key Style Rules (MUST follow)

- **Voice:** First-person experiential, senior operator tone — never AI, never consultant, never coach
- **Tone:** Declarative, calm confidence, reflective not instructional
- **US English spelling** (organization, not organisation)
- **No duplicate vocabulary** within a single piece — Marc is highly sensitive to this
- **Banned phrases:** "This resonates," "This hits close to home," "dynamic," "amazing," any templated/formulaic language
- **No emoji, no hashtag walls** (max 2-4 restrained hashtags), no "Proud to share" openings
- **Concise over verbose** — fit without "see more" when possible
- **Iterative refinement** — expect multiple passes: weave, de-duplicate, trim, voice-check
