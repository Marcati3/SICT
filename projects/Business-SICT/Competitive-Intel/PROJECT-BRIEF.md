# Competitive Intelligence Briefings

**Parent project:** Business-SICT / Silicon Craft Technology PLC

## Overview

Competitive intelligence briefings on SIC's key competitors in the RFID/NFC semiconductor space. Each briefing covers product portfolio, market positioning, financials, and strategic implications for SIC.

**Competitors covered:**
- Fudan / FMSH
- NXP (2 versions)
- Texas Instruments
- ST-Micro
