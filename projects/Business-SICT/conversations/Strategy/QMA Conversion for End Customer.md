This document defines the minimum quality management requirements governing products and services supplied by **Silicon Craft Technology Public Co., Ltd. (“SIC”)** to its **Customer**, as stipulated in the applicable contract agreement, supply agreement, or purchase order.

## 4. QUALITY MANAGEMENT REQUIREMENTS

### 4.1 Quality Management System

SIC shall establish, maintain, and operate a documented Quality Management System compliant with **ISO 9001** or an equivalent standard accepted by the Customer, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

The QMS shall be based on established operating practices and documented procedures that demonstrate a systematic approach to ensuring consistent product quality and compliance.

---

### 4.2 Information Management

SIC shall maintain documented procedures to control quality-related documents and records. These procedures shall ensure that:

- Only approved and current documents are used in production and inspection  
- Customer-provided documents are verified and controlled at point of use  
- Revision status is tracked to prevent use of obsolete documents  
- Superseded documents are removed from circulation or clearly identified if retained  

Document control shall include approval tracking, revision management, and controlled distribution to all relevant functions.

---

### 4.3 Engineering and Manufacturing Changes

SIC shall maintain formal engineering and manufacturing change control processes.

SIC shall submit a written change notification to the Customer for review and approval **prior to implementation** of any change that may affect:

- Fit, form, or function of the Product  
- Product part number or identification  
- Manufacturing process affecting Product performance  
- Packaging or labeling  
- Testing or qualification requirements  
- Manufacturing location  
- Raw materials or key inputs  

---

### 4.4 Organization and Resources

SIC shall provide suitably qualified personnel for all quality assurance and quality control activities. Quality personnel shall be trained, experienced, and certified where required.

SIC shall maintain organization charts identifying quality responsibilities and reporting lines. The Customer shall be notified in writing of any changes to key quality personnel that may affect performance or compliance.

#### 4.4.1 Training

SIC shall implement ongoing training and competency programs covering quality requirements, procedures, technical skills, and applicable regulations. Training requirements shall be documented within the QMS.

---

### 4.5 Document Control and Record Retention

SIC shall retain quality records necessary to demonstrate conformity with Customer requirements and applicable agreements.

Records shall be retained for a period meeting regulatory, contractual, or purchase order requirements, whichever is longer, and shall be made available to the Customer upon reasonable request.

### 4.6 Quality Audits

SIC shall conduct internal audits of its quality system and relevant outsourced activities in accordance with **ISO 19011**.

Audit scope and frequency shall be sufficient to ensure continued compliance. Audit results shall be documented and corrective actions implemented as required.
