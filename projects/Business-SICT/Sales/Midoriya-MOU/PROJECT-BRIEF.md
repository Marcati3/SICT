# Midoriya MOU

**Parent project:** Business-SICT / Silicon Craft Technology PLC

## Overview

Memorandum of Understanding between SIC (Silicon Craft Technology) and Midoriya Electric Co., Ltd. (Japan). Defines the terms of the commercial relationship and partnership framework for marketing SIC's industrial RFID products in Japan.

## Status

- MOU v1 drafted (5 March 2026)
- Internal review feedback received from Terry / CAE team (16 March 2026)
- MOU v2 revised to incorporate feedback (16 March 2026)
- Pending final internal approval, then Midoriya review/execution

## Key Decisions (v2 Revisions)

1. **Product scope limited to industrial sector (Phase 1)** — Midoriya to focus on promoting industrial applications only; sensor promotion deferred to Phase 2 pending RFID experience
2. **Annex I simplified** — SIC part numbers removed from Annex to reduce complexity; products listed by application category instead
3. **SIC278 reclassified** — Positioned under "Access Control" (not "Industrial RFID") to prevent Midoriya promoting it under animal identification
4. **SIC614D & SIC618A use cases clarified** — Access Control (E-Garde) and Item-Level Authentication (Tradyn, AEGID)
5. **SIC73WR adjusted** — Updated in product list per CAE team review
6. **SIC73F1 asterisk removed** — Internal check confirmed no risk on variant, engineering sample, or limited availability
7. **Phase 2 language added** — New Section 2.4 establishes conditions for expanding scope to sensor products
