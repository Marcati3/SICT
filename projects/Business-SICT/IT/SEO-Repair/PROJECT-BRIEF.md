# IT / SEO Repair — Project Brief

## Overview

Emergency SEO repair and overhaul for Silicon Craft Technology PLC website (siliconcraftth.com, WordPress). Initiated 2026-03-19 based on GA4 gap analysis of Jan-Feb 2026 data.

**Stakeholder:** Marc Spiegel (CCO)
**Prior work:** SEO Audit completed 2026-03-16 (score: 46/100) — see `../SEO-Audit/`

## Critical Failures Identified

1. **"Page Not Found" is #1 page** — 9,782 views in Jan-Feb (vs 2,866 homepage), 98.8% bounce, ~5,000 lost visitors/month
2. **96.6% bot traffic** — 43,321 of 44,853 users via (direct)/(none), Chinese cities dominate, real organic ~575 users/month
3. **Zero lead capture on datasheets** — 1,332 downloads with no form, 0.04% conversion rate
