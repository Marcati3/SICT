# Silicon Craft Technology PLC (SIC)
# https://www.sic.co.th

> Thailand's leading semiconductor and only IC design company, specializing in RFID chip and ASIC designs.

## Company Overview
Silicon Craft Technology PLC is a fabless semiconductor company listed on the Stock Exchange of Thailand (SET: SIC). Founded in 2002, the company has over 20 years of experience designing RFID, NFC, and custom ASIC solutions. SIC is a BOI A1 Tier company and the only IC design house in Thailand.

## Core Products
- **NFC Tag ICs**: SIC4310, SIC4340, SIC4341, SIC4343 — for energy harvesting, web authentication, and sensor interface
- **Industrial IoT**: ISO/IEC 15693 Tag ICs, LF HDX/FDX transponders, Multi-Protocol HF Reader ICs
- **Animal Identification**: LF transponder ICs for livestock tracking (ISO 11784/11785)
- **Immobilizer Transponders**: Automotive anti-theft IC solutions
- **Sensor Interface**: SIC7150, SIC61 family — analog front-end ICs
- **ASIC Design Services**: Custom IC design from specification to silicon

## Key Applications
- Healthcare (NFC-enabled medical devices, pH measurement)
- Brand Protection (product authentication via RFID)
- Access Control (contactless entry systems)
- Automotive (immobilizer transponders)
- Livestock Management (animal identification and tracking)
- Smart Logistics (supply chain tracking)
